About Viral Volunteers

This is a notice board for people looking to help out with the response to the COVID-19 pandemic. If you have an idea for how others can help, need to make an appeal for assistance, or you know an organization that is offering opportunities for volunteers, this site may be for you. People seeking assistance can look for services being offered by individuals and organizations. Healthcare professionals probably want to look elsewhere. Paid opportunities should not be listed. The notices are lightly curated to prevent misuse.
