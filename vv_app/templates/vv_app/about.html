About Viral Volunteers

This is a notice board for people looking to help out with the covid-19 pandemic. If you have an idea for how others can help, need to make an appeal for assistance, or you know an organization that is offering opportunities for volunteers, this site may be for you. Healthcare professionals probably want to look elsewhere. Paid opportunities should not be listed. Lightly curated to prevent misuse.
Examples:
  • Appeal - "Would someone deliver groceries to me"
  • Idea - "People should deliver groceries to their elderly neighbors"
  • Opporunity - "People who want to volunteer to deliver groceries to elderly people in their neighborhoods should register their interest"
