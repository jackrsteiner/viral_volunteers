About Viral Volunteers

This is a notice board for people looking to help out with the covid-19 pandemic. If you have an idea for how others can help, need to make an appeal for assistance, or you know an organization that is offering opportunities for volunteers, this site may be for you. This board is not for healthcare professionals or paid opportunities. It is lightly curated to prevent misuse.
